Q: what are the basic commands of Selenium ?
Ans: Below are the basic commands of Selenium:

1) Browser Commands: 
	Get  
	Title
	CurrentURL
	Close()
	Quit()

2)Navigation Command :
	Forward
	Back
	refresh

3) WebElements Commands:
	find_elements
	sendKeys()
	isDisplayed
	isSelected()
	click()
	isEnable()
	getText()
4) Action Command
	
	Switch_to_screen
	doubleclick()
	dragAndDrop()
	switch_to_frame
	move_to_element
